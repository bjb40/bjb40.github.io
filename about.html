My research looks to understand social classification, categorization, and their change over time. The focus of my work fits is population studies and medical sociology. Ive included a brief overview of my current research below.

Mortality

Our understanding of the reasons for death have changed dramatically over the last century. I am working to unerstand how much these changes--as opposed to changes in the incidence of disease--have affected our measures for causes death.

Transition to Adulthood

Can economic hardships have lasting benefits? Work in subjective well-being found that individuals who grew up during the great depression and World War II have higher subjective well-being than their children. A number of social theories propose mechanisms . This work tests these theories against the experiences of adolescents and young adults who experienced the recesson in 2007.
